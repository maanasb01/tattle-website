## Datasets We Love

This is a list of Non Tattle Datasets That Are Relevant to Social Media in India. 
This list isn't exhaustive. We have focused on datasets that account for multi-modal and multi-lingual nature of social media in India. 


### Open Access:
- *A Dataset of Fact-Checked Images Shared on WhatsApp During the Brazilian and Indian Elections*. ICWSM 2020. [Paper](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/ICWSM/article/view/7356). [Dataset.](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/ICWSM/article/view/7274)

- *Claim Matching Beyond English to Scale Global Fact-Checkings*. ACL 2021. [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.00853). [Dataset](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4890950)

- *Short is the Road that Leads from Fear to Hate: Fear Speech in Indian WhatsApp Groups.* Arxiv Preprint. [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.03870). 
[Dataset](https://github.com/hate-alert/Fear-speech-analysis/tree/master/Data)

## Available on Request:
- Sharechat Data referenced in *Characterising User Content on a Multi-Lingual Social Network*. Published at ICWSM 2020. [Paper](https://ojs.aaai.org/index.php/ICWSM/article/view/7274). [Dataset.](https://nms.kcl.ac.uk/netsys/datasets/share-chat/)
- The CoronaVirusFacts Alliance Database. [About the Alliance](https://www.poynter.org/ifcn-covid-19-misinformation/). [Database.](https://www.poynter.org/ifcn-covid-19-misinformation/)
